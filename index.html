Learn about yourself.
And contribute to research!

The In-House Identities Explorer is an online survey tool created by Prof. Richard Moorhead at the UCL Faculty of Laws. It is designed as a benchmarking tool, to provide some insight into how you see your own role, and how that compares to other in-house lawyers. In-house lawyers can use the 15-minute-questionnaire to obtain an in-depth individual report on their role orientations within their work environment.
Enter the survey tool with the password that you received via email. If you are an in-house lawyer and would like to use the tool but do not have a password to enter, please contact us by email.
You should schedule about 15 focused minutes to fill out the survey which consists of less than 100 questions. The survey can be completed on mobile and desktop without any problems.
Upon completing the survey, an individual report will be generated for you that can be exported with our email function. The report compares your score against our database and provides relevant background information. The report is personal to you. We do not hold a copy of the report or data which identifies you.

About this survey.
Designed with a research focus at University College London, the ID Explorer is a collaboration between Prof. Richard Moorhead and the Computer Science Department. Through this survey, wee hope to learn more about the role that in-house lawyers take in their respective position.
